Welcome to Ukrainian Food

We present to you some of the most popular dishes of Ukraine. Bringing a variety of Ukrainian dishes to the gastronomic capital of Europe, while making a small part of our home country available to millions of refugees from Ukraine. Our chefs come from Ukraine, they have extensive experience, also abroad.
It is a very welcoming ukrainian restaurant which:
~ Will surprise residents and tourists with its gastronomic novelties
~ Will satisfy all guests with its price and quality
~ Will unite Ukrainians and Poles even more

Catering

We cordially invite you to take advantage of our catering service.

- We have extensive experience, taking part in over 400 events in Poland and Ukraine
- We provide transport of food, straight from the production line, to the location indicated by You
- We offer a variety of choices from our box sets that look great on the table
- At the customer's special request, we are able to prepare an individual menu tailored to your needs
- If necessary, we organize waiter service and kitchen assistance
- We prepare all dishes from natural ingredients, while maintaining surprisingly low prices
