Welcome to Ukrainian Food

We present to you some of the most popular dishes of Ukraine. Bringing a variety of Ukrainian dishes to the gastronomic capital of Europe, while making a small part of our home country available to millions of refugees from Ukraine. Our chefs come from Ukraine and Belarus, they have extensive experience, also abroad.
It is a small but very welcoming restaurant which:
~ Will surprise residents and tourists with its gastronomic novelties
~ Will satisfy all guests with its price and quality
~ Will unite Ukrainians and Poles even more
